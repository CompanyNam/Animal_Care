About Us

Animal Care Club (ACC) was created to help sick or injured stray animals. Our work includes group visits to animal shelters, ecological projects, and educating people mercy towards animals in our community. The club consists of 8 curators and volunteers. We have come together to protest animal cruelty and violence, to educate young people and to rescue many lives. Our mission is to reach out our helping hand to our four-legged friends. Animal Care Club does not operate under any sponsor support. We receive charity campaigns for the material we need for the work we see. There are a number of individuals and organizations supporting our work, including Azerbaijani-French University, French Ambassador Aurelia Bouchez, Morocco Ambassador Mohammad Adil Embarsh, Icherisheher municipality, VarYox media group, Cult Baku private members club. We also want to cooperate with those who share our main goal: enthusiastic people who are concerned with animal abuse and cruelty.
